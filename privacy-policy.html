WITY ("the Extension") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect data in connection with your use of the Extension.

1. Information We Collect

WITY uses OAuth and the YouTube API to access your YouTube account with your explicit permission. We may collect and process the following types of data:

  • OAuth Authentication Data:
    • Your Google account authentication token.
    • Permissions granted to manage your YouTube account.
  • YouTube Data (via YouTube API):
    • Channel details (e.g., name, ID).
    • Video information (e.g., titles, descriptions, views).
    • Playlist details.
    • Analytics or performance data (if applicable).

Note: The Extension does not collect any other personal data from your device.

2. How We Use Your Data

The data collected is used solely to provide the functionality of WITY, including:

  • Managing your YouTube account content.
  • Offering features such as video editing, playlist management, or analytics insights.

We do not sell, share, or disclose your data to third parties, except as required by law.

3. How We Store and Protect Your Data

  • Local Storage: Data retrieved via the YouTube API is temporarily stored on your device while the Extension is active. It is not transferred to external servers.
  • Data Security: OAuth tokens are securely managed using industry-standard encryption to ensure unauthorized parties cannot access your data.

4. Your Control Over Your Data

You have full control over the data accessed by the Extension:

  • Revoke Access: You can revoke the Extension's permissions at any time via your Google Account Permissions.
  • Delete Data: If you wish to clear locally stored data, uninstalling the Extension will remove all associated information.

5. Third-Party Services

WITY uses the following third-party services:

  • YouTube API: For accessing and managing your YouTube account.
  • Google OAuth: For secure authentication.

Your use of these services is subject to their respective privacy policies:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • YouTube API Terms of Service

6. Children's Privacy

WITY is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from children.
